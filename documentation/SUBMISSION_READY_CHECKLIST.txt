KEY RESULTS SUMMARY
=============================================================

Dataset: N = 57 real comparisons (100%, no simulation)
Sources: IMPACT-HTA (n=8), BMC Medicine 2022 (n=49)

Standard Random-Effects:
  RoR = 1.020 (95% CI 0.913-1.140)
  P-value = 0.73
  I² = 91.2%
  Prediction interval: 0.50 to 2.06

Publication Bias:
  Egger's test: p < 0.001
  Trim-and-fill: 14 studies trimmed (24.6%)

Bias-Corrected Methods (Convergence):
  PEESE: RoR = 0.906 (9% underestimation)
  Trim-and-fill: RoR = 0.870 (13%)
  Limit MA: RoR = 0.867 (13%)
  Median: RoR = 0.887 (11% underestimation)
  Range: 0.867-0.906 (4% spread)

Heterogeneity Stratification:
  Concordant (DI<2, n=37): RoR=0.967, I²=0%
  Discordant (DI≥2, n=20): RoR=1.024, I²=97.7%

Concordance Sensitivity:
  DI < 1.5: 52.6%
  DI < 2.0: 64.9% (primary)
  DI < 2.5: 78.9%
  DI < 3.0: 82.5%

Bayesian MCMC:
  Posterior: RoR = 1.033 (95% CrI 0.534-2.007)
  P(underestimation) = 45.7% (inconclusive)

Selection Models:
  Failed (N<100, high heterogeneity)
  Diagnostic analysis provided
